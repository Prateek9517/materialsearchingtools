There are three categories of ferrous metal, which ferrous metal are you looking for:

Steel is a combination of two elements, iron, and carbon. Steel is broken into four types and then in those types steel is broken down into grades. Steel is used for making knives, tools, machines, etc.

Cast Iron, is an alloy of iron that contains 2 to 4 percent carbon, along with varying amounts of silicon and manganese and traces of impurities such as sulfur and phosphorus. It is made by reducing iron ore in a blast furnace.

Wrought iron is a soft, ductile, fibrous variety that is produced from a semi-fused mass of relatively pure iron globules partially surrounded by slag. It usually contains less than 0.1 percent carbon and 1 or 2 percent slag.
