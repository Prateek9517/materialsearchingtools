There are three categories of Steel, which steel are you looking for:

Stainless steel is an alloy of iron that is resistant to rusting and corrosion. It contains elements such as carbon, other nonmetals, and metals to obtain other desired properties.

Alloy steel is steel that is alloyed with a variety of elements in total amounts between 1.0% and 50% by weight to improve its mechanical properties. Alloy steels used in hydraulic steel structures are usually cast or forged as well.

Carbon steel is a steel with carbon content from about 0.05 up to 2.1 percent by weight. Carbon steel is a popular metal choice for knife-making due to its high amount of carbon, giving the blade more edge retention.
